# Executive Sponsorship Research

Source: https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-bi/guidance/fabric-adoption-roadmap-executive-sponsorship

## Role of Executive Sponsor
- Formulating strategic vision, goals, and priorities
- Providing top-down guidance and reinforcement
- Leading by example
- Allocating staffing and resources
- Approving funding (Fabric licenses)
- Removing barriers
- Decision-making for governance
- Dispute resolution
- Supporting change initiatives

## Identification Patterns
- **Top-down**: CEO hires CDO/CAO or empowers existing exec (CFO)
- **Bottom-up**: Emerges from business unit success, grows into role

## Maturity Levels
| Level | State |
|-------|-------|
| 100 | Awareness from some executives, but no sponsor or decision-maker identified |
| 200 | Informal executive support through informal channels |
| 300 | Executive sponsor identified with clear expectations |
| 400 | Well-established sponsor with cross-org authority, productive partnerships |
| 500 | Highly engaged sponsor driving data culture vision, using KPIs/OKRs |
