# Content Ownership & Management Research

Source: https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-bi/guidance/fabric-adoption-roadmap-content-ownership-and-management

## Three Strategies
1. **Business-led self-service**: Business users own and manage content end-to-end
2. **Managed self-service**: Centralized data, decentralized reporting. IT manages data layer, business creates reports
3. **Enterprise BI**: IT/central team owns and manages everything end-to-end

## Key Considerations
- Most orgs use a blend of all three strategies
- "Managed self-service" is often the ideal balance
- Content ownership affects governance, COE model, and adoption

## Maturity Levels
| Level | State |
|-------|-------|
| 100 | Chaotic, no coordination, duplicate work, no standards |
| 200 | Recognizing need for balance, some standards emerging |
| 300 | Defined approach with clear ownership model, roles understood |
| 400 | Clear accountability, partnership between business and IT, governance guardrails |
| 500 | Well-documented, self-service and enterprise coexist seamlessly, continuous improvement |
