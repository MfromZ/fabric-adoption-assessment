# Community of Practice Research

Source: https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-bi/guidance/fabric-adoption-roadmap-community-of-practice

## Definition
Group of people with common interest who interact and help each other voluntarily.

## Key Components
- **Champions network**: Go-to experts in business units who help colleagues
- **Knowledge sharing**: Discussion channels, lunch & learn, office hours, blog posts, user groups
- **Incentives**: Contests, ranking, leadership communication, specialized training
- **Communication plan**: New employee comms, onboarding, ongoing, situational

## Maturity Levels
| Level | State |
|-------|-------|
| 100 | Some creators doing great work but unrecognized, rare knowledge sharing, inconsistent communication |
| 200 | First champions identified, goals for champions network, knowledge sharing gaining traction |
| 300 | Knowledge sharing is normal and frequent, communication goals defined |
| 400 | Champions in all business units, incentives common, regular communication per plan |
| 500 | Bidirectional feedback loops, KPIs measure engagement, automation adds value |
