# Content Delivery Scope Research

Source: https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-bi/guidance/fabric-adoption-roadmap-content-delivery-scope

## Four Delivery Scopes
1. **Personal**: Individual use (exploration, learning)
2. **Team**: Small group collaboration
3. **Departmental**: Entire department or business unit
4. **Enterprise**: Organization-wide, critical content

## Key Points
- Governance expectations increase as scope widens
- Personal → minimal governance; Enterprise → strict governance
- The scope affects workspace management, security, endorsements

## Maturity Levels
| Level | State |
|-------|-------|
| 100 | No clarity on content scope, no differentiated governance |
| 200 | Some awareness of different scopes, initial efforts to organize workspaces |
| 300 | Clear workspace strategies per scope, governance expectations defined per scope |
| 400 | Well-managed delivery across all scopes, appropriate governance at each level |
| 500 | Optimized delivery with automation, clear lifecycle management for all scopes |
